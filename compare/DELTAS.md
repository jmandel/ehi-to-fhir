## Notes on classification heuristics

- **isomorphic-ref**: reference resolves to the same natural key on both sides (id differs only).
- **specificity-ref**: our referent is same-or-narrower than target, VERIFIED by a parent/child
  join in the EHI org/location tree (CLARITY_DEP/CLARITY_SA). Unjoinable brand/department pairs
  fall to **unsure** (candidate for a BLESSED per-case attestation, not auto-toleration).
- **cosmetic-display**: display/text equal after normalize, or nickname/abbreviation containment.
- **coding-gap**: a missing standard coding (LOINC/SNOMED/RxNorm/ICD/CVX/CPT) — own bucket.
- **real-gap**: a changed scalar or a value present in target but missing from ours.
- **unsure**: needs human eyes (different-entity ref, divergent display, or our-only element).
